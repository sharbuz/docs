# What is AI/Run CodeMie?

AI/Run CodeMie is the dynamic AI-agents platform revolutionizing the way businesses approach project management, business analysis, development, testing, devops, and more, through the power of AI-assistants. In today’s competitive and fast-paced software development environment, efficiency and automation are key. CodeMie is crafted to meet these needs by enabling users to create and customize AI-assistants tailored to their specific project requirements.

CodeMie is not just about automation; it's about smart automation. It allows users to design AI-assistants capable of performing actual tasks – from creating issues in Jira to managing pull requests in Git. This hands-on approach ensures that the AI-assistants do not merely provide suggestions but take part in the workflow, executing tasks that traditionally required manual effort.

The platform's versatility extends across various domains within software development and project management, ensuring that whether you're analyzing business requirements, leading development teams, or overseeing quality assurance, there's an AI-assistant that can be customized to your needs.

CodeMie stands as a testament to the potential of AI in enhancing productivity and streamlining operations. It's built for businesses looking to leverage the latest in AI technology to not only automate their processes but also to innovate how these processes are executed. With CodeMie, we're moving towards a future where AI-assistants are integral to the development lifecycle, making software projects more efficient, less error-prone, and faster to market.
